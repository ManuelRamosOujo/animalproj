Seals

This animal is an aquatic mammal , it eats another animals and has an overall round shape. Most of them live in the Artic and Antartic ocean, but they can be found in most coasts around the world.

They hunt:

  1. Fish
  2. Crustaceans
  3. Seabirds
  4. Other seals (only leopard seals do it)

  • Elephant seal
